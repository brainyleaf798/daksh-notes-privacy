Privacy Policy for Daksh Notes

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Daksh Notes ("the app") is a simple note-taking application. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Daksh Notes does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or to any third party. All notes you create are stored locally on your device only.

Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection and does not send any data over the internet.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your camera, contacts, location, microphone, storage, or any other device permissions.

Advertising and Analytics

Daksh Notes does not display ads and does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored only on your device, you can delete your notes at any time directly within the app, or remove all app data by uninstalling Daksh Notes from your device.

Children's Privacy

Daksh Notes does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13, as the app does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future (for example, if new features are added), this page will be updated accordingly.
